Chamberlin's Ole Forest Inn Accommodations

Welcome to Chamberlin's Ole Forest Inn of Curtis, Michigan. We invite you to become one of the many people who return time and again to this premier lakeside country inn with 19th century charm and modern-day convenience. Wander up the beautiful open stairway to the second and third floors, where twelve distinctive guest rooms await you with warm chenille spreads, country quilts and glorious antiques.
