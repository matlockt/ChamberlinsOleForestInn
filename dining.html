Dining at the Inn

Casual Fine Dining

Offering a variety of tasty fare, classic cuisine, cocktails and specialty drinks. Come and enjoy dinner by the fireside in our Lakeview Dining Room; our Forest Room is the perfect place for groups to gather whether for a private dinner party or quiet meeting area. The Pub's libations, sports and tales are tops, and warm weather can best be enjoyed on the Veranda with quaint outdoor seating and breathtaking views.

The Lakeside Dining Room boasts a beautiful 10 foot stone fireplace, spacious seating with stunning views of Big Manistique Lake and live musical entertainment is often an added pleasure.

The Forest Room is our private dining room perfect for groups to gather for a quiet meeting area or private dinner party (we have wireless internet and a projector screen is available upon request for meetings).

The whitefish is fresh, the prime rib is oven roasted, and our full menu includes nightly specials.

The Pub where flavor and fun can be found in this quaint area of the Inn with libations, sports and tales for all to enjoy. Any time of year...come and relax with us!

Outdoor Dining

The wrap-around Veranda is the perfect place to enjoy warm weather and outdoor dining; exhilarate in the breathtaking view of the huge island-studded Big Manistique Lake with loons, eagles and spectacular sunsets.
